Featured Games
Slug Slasher is a 2D action platformer where the player takes on the role of a chef who must defend the world from an onslaught of giant slugs with only his trusty bucket of salt.

The game is currently in development and can be found at slugslasher.com
One Run Dungeon is an arcade−style side-scroller with one goal: beat the game in one run. Armed with just your spellbook and staff, you must defeat countless hordes sent by the dungeon's master.

The game is up on Steam Greenlight now!

Hoosier Games kicks off each semester with a two hour long game jam where participants try to make a game from a randomly generated title in teams of four to six people of different roles.
